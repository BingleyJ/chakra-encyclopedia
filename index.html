What Are Chakras?

Chakras are energy centers that govern specific functions of the body and mind. When balanced, they promote physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being.

Each chakra radiates a specific color and energy, corresponding to glands in the physical body. When balanced, they support our physical, emotional, psychological, and spiritual well-being.

लं
CHAKRA 1

Root Chakra (Muladhara)

The foundation of the chakra system, promoting stability, grounding, and security. Located at the base of the spine, it connects you to your roots and serves as the base for energy supply to all other chakras.

Attributes:

  • Color: Red
  • Element: Earth
  • Mantra: Lam
  • Gland: Adrenal glands

When Balanced:

  • Feeling grounded and secure
  • Physical health and vitality
  • Financial stability
  • Trust in the world
वं
CHAKRA 2

Sacral Chakra (Svadhisthana)

The seat of creativity, emotions, and personal pleasure. Located in the lower abdomen, it represents the residence of one's feelings and desires, governing creativity, sexuality, and emotional flow.

Attributes:

  • Color: Orange
  • Element: Water
  • Mantra: Vam
  • Gland: Gonads

When Balanced:

  • Creative expression
  • Emotional intelligence
  • Healthy relationships
  • Joy and pleasure
रं
CHAKRA 3

Solar Plexus Chakra (Manipura)

The "city of jewels" and center of personal power, confidence, and self-esteem. Located in the upper abdomen, it's the shining core of inner strength, willpower, and personal identity.

Attributes:

  • Color: Yellow
  • Element: Fire
  • Mantra: Ram
  • Gland: Pancreas

When Balanced:

  • Confidence and self-esteem
  • Personal power
  • Clear decision-making
  • Productivity and focus
यं
CHAKRA 4

Heart Chakra (Anahata)

The "unstruck" center representing pure, untainted space of love and compassion. Located in the center of the chest, it's the bridge between lower and upper chakras, governing unconditional love and emotional healing.

Attributes:

  • Color: Green
  • Element: Air
  • Mantra: Yam
  • Gland: Thymus

When Balanced:

  • Unconditional love
  • Compassion and empathy
  • Emotional balance
  • Deep connections
हं
CHAKRA 5

Throat Chakra (Vishuddha)

The "thoroughly purified" center of communication and self-expression. Located in the throat, it represents the purity of voice and truth, helping you express thoughts and bring inner desires to manifestation.

Attributes:

  • Color: Blue
  • Element: Ether/Space
  • Mantra: Ham
  • Gland: Thyroid

When Balanced:

  • Clear communication
  • Authentic expression
  • Active listening
  • Creative voice
ॐ
CHAKRA 6

Third Eye Chakra (Ajna)

The "command" center of intuition, insight, and inner vision. Located between the eyebrows, it guides perception and understanding beyond ordinary senses, representing wisdom and devotion.

Attributes:

  • Color: Indigo
  • Element: Light
  • Mantra: Om
  • Gland: Pituitary

When Balanced:

  • Intuitive insights
  • Clear vision
  • Wisdom and clarity
  • Spiritual awareness
ॐ
CHAKRA 7

Crown Chakra (Sahasrara)

The "thousand-petaled" highest chakra representing enlightenment and spiritual connection. Located at the crown of the head, it signifies the ultimate realization of divine consciousness and universal connection.

Attributes:

  • Color: Violet/White
  • Element: Thought/Consciousness
  • Mantra: Silence
  • Gland: Pineal

When Balanced:

  • Spiritual connection
  • Higher consciousness
  • Inner peace
  • Universal wisdom
