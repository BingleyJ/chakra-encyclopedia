Social Media Kit

Ready-to-use templates and content for promoting your chakra encyclopedia

Platform Strategy

Pinterest Strategy

  • Post chakra infographics daily
  • Create "7 Chakra Healing" boards
  • Use tall vertical images (2:3 ratio)
  • Include detailed descriptions with keywords
  • Pin to group boards for reach
Best times: 8-11 PM
Frequency: 5-10 pins/day

Instagram Strategy

  • Share beautiful chakra graphics
  • Post meditation reels and stories
  • Use carousel posts for education
  • Hashtags: #chakras #meditation #healing
  • Engage with wellness accounts
Best times: 6-9 AM, 7-9 PM
Frequency: 1-2 posts/day

YouTube Strategy

  • Create guided meditation videos
  • Chakra educational content
  • Use SEO-optimized titles
  • Include affiliate links in descriptions
  • Create playlists by chakra
Best times: 2-4 PM
Frequency: 1-2 videos/week
